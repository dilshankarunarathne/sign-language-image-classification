Welcome to SLSignDetect

About

Our web application is designed to effortlessly interpret Sinhala Sign Language gestures, providing a seamless bridge for communication within the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. With real-time gesture recognition, user-friendly interface, and educational resources, SLSignDetect aims to make Sinhala Sign Language more accessible and inclusive. Join us in breaking down communication barriers and fostering a world where everyone can express themselves with ease.
